GOOGLE FORMS OPTION (EASIEST - NO CODING!)
===========================================

This is the SIMPLEST option - uses Google Forms which automatically saves to Google Sheets.
No coding, no API setup, works immediately!

STEP 1: Create a Google Form
-----------------------------
1. Go to https://forms.google.com/
2. Click the "+" to create a new form
3. Title: "Navi-ken Waiting List"
4. Add these questions:
   - Question 1: "Full Name" (Short answer, Required)
   - Question 2: "Email" (Short answer, Required)
   - Question 3: "Phone Number" (Short answer)
   - Question 4: "Country" (Dropdown with options: Japan, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Other)
   - Question 5: "How did you hear about us?" (Multiple choice: Website, Social Media, Friend, Event, News, Other)
   - Question 6: "Additional Comments" (Paragraph)

STEP 2: Customize the Form Design
----------------------------------
1. Click the palette icon at the top
2. Choose a color scheme (purple to match your website)
3. Click "Preview" to see how it looks

STEP 3: Get the Form Link
--------------------------
1. Click "Send" button at the top
2. Click the link icon (<>)
3. Click "COPY" to copy the form URL
4. It will look like: https://forms.gle/XXXXXXXX

STEP 4: Update Your Website Button
-----------------------------------
Open index.html and change the button link:

From:
<a href="signup.html" class="waiting-list-btn" data-translate="waiting_list_btn">

To:
<a href="YOUR_GOOGLE_FORM_LINK" target="_blank" class="waiting-list-btn" data-translate="waiting_list_btn">

STEP 5: View Responses (Automatic Excel Export!)
-------------------------------------------------
1. In your Google Form, click "Responses" tab
2. Click the green Sheets icon to create a spreadsheet
3. All responses will automatically appear in this Google Sheet
4. To export to Excel: File → Download → Microsoft Excel (.xlsx)

STEP 6: Email Notifications
----------------------------
1. In your Google Form, click the three dots menu
2. Select "Get email notifications for new responses"
3. You'll receive an email every time someone submits!
